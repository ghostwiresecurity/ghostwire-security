Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer free trials?

Yes! Ghost and AuditVault come with 30-day free trials. No credit card required. OTWI pricing is custom but we can provide demo access.

What size organizations do you work with?

We primarily serve small-to-medium MSPs, SOC teams, and DoD contractors. Our tools are built to be affordable and practical for teams of 1-50 people.

Can you integrate with our existing tools?

Ghost integrates with major SIEM/EDR platforms (Splunk, Arctic Wolf, Huntress, etc.). AuditVault connects to most compliance and ticketing systems. Let's discuss your specific stack on a demo call.

Yes, especially for OTWI and federal customers. We can discuss deployment options based on your security requirements.

All products include email support. Priority phone/Slack support is available for enterprise customers. As a small company, you're talking directly to the person who built the tools.
