Our Services

Ghostwire Security offers comprehensive cybersecurity solutions tailored to your organization's needs. We believe in practical, effective security that doesn't require a PhD to understand.

Security Assessments

We evaluate your current security posture and identify vulnerabilities before attackers do. Our assessments are thorough yet understandable, providing clear recommendations you can actually implement.

Penetration Testing

Think like an attacker to defend like a professional. We simulate real-world attacks to test your defenses and help you strengthen weak points.

Security Training

Your people are your first line of defense. We provide engaging, practical training that helps your team recognize and respond to security threats effectively.

Incident Response

When security incidents occur, every minute counts. We provide rapid response services to help you contain, investigate, and recover from security breaches.

Consulting Services

Need strategic guidance on your security program? We help you develop practical security strategies that align with your business goals and resources.

Compliance Assistance

Navigate security compliance requirements with confidence. We help you understand and meet regulatory standards without the complexity.
