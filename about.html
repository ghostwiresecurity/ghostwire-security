Our Story

Ghostwire Security was born from frustration.

After years working as a SOC analyst managing security for 30+ client environments, I kept hitting the same problems: drowning in false positive alerts, spending weeks on compliance paperwork, and lacking the threat intelligence needed to protect critical infrastructure.

The existing tools either cost too much for small MSPs, were too complex for actual analysts to use, or just didn't solve the right problems.

So I built the tools I wished I had.

Ghost cuts alert triage time by 60%. AuditVault turns 40 hours of compliance work into 5 minutes. OTWI delivers actionable intelligence for organizations that can't afford a dedicated threat intel team.

Every feature we build solves a problem we've personally experienced in production environments. No bloat, no complexity for complexity's sake. Just tools that work.

— Jeremy Ellis, Founder
